Introduction

My name is Daniel Tello-Torres. I am a sophomore attending Michigan State University majoring in Experience Architecture. My goal is to contribute to physical and digital environments by improving user experience so users can navigate easily while also being able to use and access what they are looking for.


I want to broaden my skills so that I can help communities from all over the world. I want to show my work experiences and how they can be applied in my field of study so that I can adapt new skills and find a career that I can work with in the future. Along with that, I want to get out of my comfort zone by interacting with others and working with them to meet our goals.


Welcome to my portfolio where I will show you who I am, what I have done so far, and my goals that I want to accomplish!

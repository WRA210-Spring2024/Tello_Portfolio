My Work




Wireframe Design for UberEats: This project is a piece of work I have worked on during my free time to practice wireframing. The key skills I focused on were designing and editing. I focused on making the page a lot less cluttered where it would take endless scrolling to find certain options, which would have been overwhelming for users. I prioritized making the page more simple by adding the search bar in the middle along with displaying recently purchased items on the bottom. This brought improvement to my profession by looking from the perspective of users and thinking of simple designs to navigate the website more easily.







Photo Editing using Adobe Photoshop: I worked on a couple of pictures that I chose for this project. The objective was to learn and apply the skills taught in the class to apply to any three pictures of our choice. I learned about color grading, removing unwanted elements, manipulating photos using effects and filters, typography, and more.
